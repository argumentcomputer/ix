# Cold circuit groups

Data: main-branch per-circuit stats from `ix check` on `Nat.add_comm`
(`nat_add_comm_result`, total FFT cost 53.9M) and `String.append`
(`string_append_result`, total FFT cost 967.6M).

- **FFT cost** of a source function = the sum over all of its
  monomorphized instances, per workload.
- **cold** = FFT cost at most 0.1% of the workload total in *both*
  workloads. Unlike a fixed call-count cutoff, the share-based rule is
  invariant to the size of the computation being proven. 640 of 711
  non-`pub` functions qualify; 71 stay hot.
- **width** = max singleton circuit width over the function's instances.
- Groups are width bands, so members of a group waste little of the
  merged circuit's shared (max-sized) column region.
